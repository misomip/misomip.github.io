The first phase of submissions to MISOMIP2 is now open. At this stage, we are asking participants to submit results to the **OceanA-hind & OceanW-hind** experiments. Calls for submission to the other experiments will follow later.

A short description of the experiments can be found [here](/misomip2_dir/misomip2_experiments.md), with further details available in the [protocol manuscript](https://gmd.copernicus.org/articles/17/7105/2024/).

# MISOMIP2 Contribution Submission Instructions

Please follow these steps to submit your MISOMIP2 data contribution:

1. **Data preparation**  
   Organize your simulation output files according to the [MISOMIP2 data format guidelines](https://gmd.copernicus.org/articles/17/7105/2024/).  

2. **Create a personal Zenodo archive**  
   Upload your data to a new archive on [Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/).

3. **Choose archive visibility**  
   - **Public:** The archive will be openly accessible, allowing the MISOMIP2 coordination team to download your data.  
   - **Private:** The archive will be restricted. If you select this option, please **send us the private link** so we can access your files.  

4. **Submit your archive link**  
   Send the Zenodo archive URL to the MISOMIP2 coordination team via email (or the designated submission platform).  

5. **Confirmation**  
   We will acknowledge receipt of your data and confirm access shortly.
